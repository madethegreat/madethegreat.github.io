About Me

I am a student in Physics at the UM-DAE Centre for Excellence in Basic Sciences, Mumbai, with strong interests in ultracold atoms , quantum optics and AMO physics .

Working on a summer project where I wrote code for the data analysis of BEC images first got me interested in AMO physics. I picked up the basics of laser locking and ECDL along the way and am currently looking for projects to gain experimental experience in AMO physics. Before figuring out my interest and desire to work in the field of ultracold atoms, I have worked on a lot of other computational projects. I enjoy numerical methods and scientific computation as well.
